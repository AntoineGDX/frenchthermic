Soft, elevated surface for content blocks, service tiles and panels.

```jsx
<Card hoverable accent>
  <h3>Audit énergétique</h3>
  <p>Évaluez la performance de votre logement.</p>
</Card>
```

`elevation` sets shadow depth; `accent` adds a terracotta top keyline for featured cards; `hoverable` lifts on hover; `padding` controls inner space.
